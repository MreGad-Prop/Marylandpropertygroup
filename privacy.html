Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Who we are

Maryland Property Group is a trade name of More Gad LLC ("Maryland Property Group," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information in connection with this website, marylandpropertygroup.com (the "Site"), and the inquiries and requests you submit through it.

This policy applies only to the Site. It does not cover information you provide to third parties through their own platforms, including the AppFolio property management system we use for residents and owners, which operates under AppFolio's own privacy practices.

Information we collect

Information you submit through forms

When you contact us or complete a form on the Site, you may choose to provide information such as:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number;
  • A property address and details about the property;
  • Occupancy status, the condition of the property, and your timeline;
  • Investor details when you request investor access;
  • The content of any message or notes you include with your request.

You provide this information voluntarily. You can choose not to submit a form, although doing so may limit our ability to respond to your inquiry.

Technical and usage data

When you submit a form, our hosting and form pipeline may process limited technical information automatically, such as your IP address and browser or user-agent string. This information helps us deliver your message, maintain the security of the Site, and prevent abuse and fraud.

Information handled outside the Site

Rent payments and resident or owner portal data are handled within AppFolio, not collected by this Site. That information is processed in accordance with AppFolio's own privacy practices. If you interact with AppFolio, we encourage you to review the privacy terms presented there.

How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you;
  • Prepare and discuss proposals;
  • Evaluate potential property purchases;
  • Review requests for investor access;
  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services and the Site;
  • Protect the security of the Site and guard against fraud and misuse.

Legal basis and consent

We process your information based on your consent and on our legitimate business interests in operating, securing, and improving our services and in responding to the requests you send us. You submit forms and provide your information voluntarily, and you may decline to do so.

How we share information

We share personal information only as needed to operate our business and serve you. This may include sharing with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the Site and our communications, such as Cloudflare for hosting, an email delivery provider, and AppFolio for property management;
  • Authorities or other parties when required to comply with applicable law, legal process, or enforceable governmental requests;
  • A successor entity in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or other business transfer.

We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies and analytics

The Site is designed to use minimal, essential functionality. If privacy-friendly analytics are enabled, they are intended to be aggregate and cookieless, meaning they do not rely on tracking cookies and are not used to identify you individually. We do not use your information for cross-site behavioral advertising.

Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, or for as long as required to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or de-identify it.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices and rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have submitted to us by emailing us at the address in the Contact section below. We will respond consistent with applicable law.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights. For example, residents of certain states, including California, Virginia, and Maryland, may have rights to access, correct, delete, or limit certain uses of their personal information, and to be free from discrimination for exercising those rights. To exercise any available rights, email us using the Contact information below, and we will verify and process your request as required by applicable law.

Children's privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Third-party links

The Site may contain links to third-party websites and services, such as AppFolio. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we handle personal information.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise any available rights, please contact us at ben@marylandpropertygroup.com.
